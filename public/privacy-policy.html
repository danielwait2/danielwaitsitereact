Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Daniel Wait and Wait Works LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at danielwait.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We collect anonymous analytics data to understand how visitors use our website. This includes:

  • Page views - Which pages you visit and how long you stay
  • Session data - How you navigate through our site
  • Geographic information - Country, region, and city (no specific addresses)
  • Device information - Browser type, screen size, and device type
  • Referrer data - How you found our website

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage for:

  • Admin authentication - To maintain your login session for admin functions
  • Analytics tracking - To identify unique visitors and sessions
  • Site preferences - To remember your light/dark mode preference

Contact Information

If you contact us via email (daniel@waitworks.com), we may collect your email address and any information you choose to share in your message.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Website improvement - Understanding how visitors use our site to make it better
  • Content optimization - Identifying popular pages and content
  • Technical support - Responding to your inquiries and providing assistance
  • Security - Protecting against unauthorized access to admin functions

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Cloudflare Workers - For processing and storing analytics data
  • Cloudflare KV - For secure data storage
  • HTTPS encryption - All data transmission is encrypted
  • Access controls - Admin functions are password-protected

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Analytics data is used internally only for website improvement purposes. We may share information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request information about what data we have about you
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Opt-out - Disable analytics tracking in your browser settings

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare - For hosting, security, and analytics processing
  • Google Fonts - For website typography
  • Bootstrap Icons - For website icons

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
