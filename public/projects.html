Experience iOS App

I created "Experience," an iOS app built in Swift that captures and transcribes temple visit experiences through voice recordings. It features automatic transcription, temple selection, and fields for participants. I designed it to allow users to easily review, edit, and filter entries by date or type, with all data stored locally for convenience.
